# The Foundation Guard

"The Foundation Guard" is a modern, industrial-styled take on the classic maze-defense genre. You play as a contractor tasked with protecting the integrity of a maze being relentlessly dismantled by industrial monsters.

## Core Mechanics

### 1. Combat & Defense
- **Punch (F)**: A quick, low-damage attack that interrupts monster actions.
- **Kick (E)**: A high-knockback attack that pushes monsters away.
- **Squeeze**: Pin a monster against a wall and keep walking into it to deal continuous environmental damage.
- **Maze Integrity**: Monsters destroy walls to reduce the global Maze Integrity. If it hits 0%, you lose an Insurance Strike.

### 2. Progression
- **Levels**: Defeat all 4 monsters in a level to immediately advance to the next.
- **Scaling Difficulty**: With each level, monsters destroy the maze 50% faster, and their attacks become more frequent.
- **Player Health**: Monsters can attack you back. Watch your health bar in the HUD!

## Tech Stack
- **Engine**: Matter.js (Physics)
- **Framework**: Vite + TypeScript
- **Styling**: Vanilla CSS with an industrial aesthetic.
